    Our History

    In 2024,Sumi Coffee shop was born from a simple yet powerful dream—to create a space where coffee lovers could come together and savor rich, handcrafted blends. What started as a humble café soon became a beloved destination, where every cup is made with passion, precision, and a deep love for the art of brewing.

    From the moment we opened our doors, we’ve been committed to providing an exceptional coffee experience. We source the finest beans, embrace sustainable practices, and continuously innovate to bring you flavorsthat delight and inspire.
    Whether you’re here for a peaceful morning ritual or a lively conversation over lattes, our shop is built on the warmth of community.
